Diwali

Diwali festival

Diwali is primarily celebrated in India, where it holds significant cultural, religious, and social importance. It is one of the most widely observed festivals in the country and is celebrated by people of various religious and cultural backgrounds. Additionally, Diwali is also celebrated by Indian communities around the world, making it a global festival. In regions with a significant Indian diaspora, such as Nepal, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Singapore, and various countries in the Middle East, North America, Europe, and beyond, Diwali is observed with enthusiasm and various cultural traditions.
